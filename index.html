About Me

Hi, I am Majed Samyal. I started my professional career back in April 2010, with a small software development company. It was just a 2-year old company with only four software developers, named Xpertz Softtech located in Chandigarh.

I have not those good academic records, the highest qualification which I hold is Bachelors Of Computer Application in distance mode from well reputed University in Distance learning, IGNOU. In India, the software industry doesn't entertain candidates having distance education for a job with no experience. As Xpertz Softtect was an emerging company, they hired me as a software trainee. Till now I feel obliged to the interviewee and my first guide for trusting and supporting me at the start of my career.

I had admitted, I am a failure, after getting started, I never looked back.

So I'm Inn, the immense and scaled IT industry of India, the journey is going on. Present days, I am working with an MNC named Wolters Kluwer, it's a product based company, they have their product development center in Pune, Maharashtra.

I started my career in Microsoft stack when Web Forms was the heart of Web. The industry was moving towards Web applications from Windows applications. Linq was rising and so Linq to SQL ORM. Later on, everyone starts talking about ASP.NET MVC, the new way of developing Web Applications. I have more than two years of experience working in Asp.Net MVC.
By 2013, Single Page Applications on the rising, Angular and other JavaScript frameworks start ruling the industry, yes we can call this a hype. I have in-depth knowledge of JavaScript, more than 6 months of hand on experience of working in Angular JS. I believe the fact that when you know the mother language of the framework, it takes a few days to be productive in that framework.

In my current organization, I am working on Tax & Accounting domain product. It's an enterprise application with a lot of legacy code, which means most of the time spent on reading code rather than writing. An advantage of working in the legacy code is that you can understand how a well-written code can save time and money in the long run. I am aware of the basic principles of Software Development. The SOLID Principle, some frequently used design patterns, the fundamentals of the Object-Oriented paradigm, the basics of Functional programming.

To Summarize the technology stack, I have experience of working in C#.Net, Asp.Net MVC, JavaScript, NoSql, MySql, Linq, Entity Framework. Good understanding of Design Patterns and Principles, the OOPS fundamentals, Test Driven Development, and experience of working in Agile methodology.

These days I am reading more about Domain Driven Design, and trying my hands on Azure and Docker. Reading is my hobby, I am interested in reading about Cosmology.
